About Me:

Top 3 Hobbies:

  • Playing with my kids
  • Landscape Photography
  • Camping/hiking

I grew up in Minnesota with a family that loved to travel. We spent a week each year camping, another week each year skiing, and another week each year at the EAA Oshkosh fly-in. During college and after graduation, I continued to travel, including Germany, Russia, Turkey, and New Zealand.

After that period, I settled down back in Minnesota for abut 5 years, until I met my wife. We moved to Seattle, got engaged then married. That's when I started looking for a career in earnest.

After several stints in small companies, I began attending Code Fellows to become a software developer, which is where I am now. I love it and am learning a lot every day.
